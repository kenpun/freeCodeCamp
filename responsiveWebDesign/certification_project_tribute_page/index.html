Michael Pollan

Bestselling Author & Journalist

Here is a short bio of Michael Pollan's work:

  • 1995 - present - Contributing writer, New York Times Magazine
  • 2003 - present - James S. and John L. Knight Professor of of Journalism
  • 2018 - Book: How to Change Your Mind: What the New Science of PsychedelicsTeaches Us About Consciousness, Dying, Addiction, Depression, and Transcendence (The Penguin Press)
  • 2010 - Book: Food Rules: An Eater's Manual (The Penguin Press)
  • 2013 - Book: Cooked: A Natural History of Transformation (The Penguin Press)
  • 2008 - Book: - In Defense of Food: An Eater's Manifesto (The Penguin Press)
  • 2006 - Book: The Omnivore's Dilemma: A Natural History of Four Meals (The Penguin Press)
  • 2001 - Book: The Botany of Desire: A Plant's-Eye View of the World (Random House)
